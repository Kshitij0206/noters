Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Noters.online. If you can’t find your answer here, email us at support@noters.online.

General

  1. What is Noters.online? — A cloud-based note-taking app with advanced features like tags, pinning, voice-to-text, and more.
  2. Is Noters.online free? — Yes, the core features are free. Premium features may be added later.
  3. Do I need to sign up to use it? — You can use guest mode, but logging in with Google unlocks syncing and extra features.
  4. Does it work on mobile? — Yes, it’s fully responsive and mobile-friendly.
  5. Which browsers are supported? — Latest versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
  6. Can I use it on a tablet? — Yes, the layout adapts to all screen sizes.
  7. Does Noters have an app? — Not yet, but it’s planned for future releases.

Creating & Managing Notes

  1. How do I create a new note? — Click the “New Note” button and start typing.
  2. How do I edit an existing note? — Open the note from “Saved Notes” and click “Edit”.
  3. Can I change a note’s background color? — Yes, use the background color picker in the editor.
  4. How do I pin a note? — Click the 📌 icon next to the note.
  5. Can I tag notes? — Yes, enter tags in the “Tags” field when creating or editing a note.
  6. How do I search notes by tags? — Use the tag filter in “Saved Notes”.
  7. Can I sort notes? — Yes, by date, title, or pinned status.
  8. Is drag-and-drop supported? — Yes, in “Saved Notes” you can reorder notes manually.
  9. How do I delete a note? — Click the 🗑️ icon and confirm deletion.

Advanced Features

  1. Can I export notes as PDF? — Yes, click “Download as PDF” on the note page.
  2. How do I enable dark mode? — Toggle the 🌙 icon in the top-right corner to switch themes instantly.
  3. Does Noters have voice-to-text? — Yes, click the 🎤 icon in the editor.
  4. Can I lock notes? — Yes, set a password when creating or editing a note.
  5. Is there version history? — Yes, you can view and restore older versions of a note.
  6. Can I upload images in notes? — Yes, use the “Insert Image” option in the editor.
  7. Does Noters summarize notes? — Yes, click “Summarize” to generate a condensed version.
  8. Can I format text? — Yes, using the built-in toolbar for headings, bold, italics, and more.

Account & Login

  1. How do I log in? — Use “Continue with Google” for quick access.
  2. Can I change my email? — Not currently; email support for changes.
  3. What if I forget my password? — We use Google login, so reset your password via Google.
  4. Can I delete my account? — Yes, email support@noters.online.
  5. Is my data private? — Yes, we use encryption and never sell your data.
  6. Can I sync across devices? — Yes, when logged in with Google.
  7. How do I log out? — Click your profile icon and select “Logout”.
  8. Can I have multiple accounts? — Yes, but each requires a different Google login.
  9. Will my notes be deleted if I log out? — No, they remain saved in your account.
  10. Does Noters support two-factor authentication? — Not yet, but it’s planned.

Troubleshooting

  1. Notes are not saving! — Check your internet connection.
  2. Dark mode isn’t working? — Make sure your browser supports CSS variables and that localStorage is enabled. Try refreshing after toggling.
  3. Voice-to-text is not responding? — Allow microphone permissions in your browser.
  4. Images won’t upload? — Ensure the file is under 5MB and in JPG/PNG format.
  5. Tags aren’t showing? — Ensure you save after adding tags.
  6. PDF download is blank? — Refresh and try again; large notes may take time.
  7. Site looks broken? — Clear browser cache or try incognito mode.
  8. Editor toolbar is missing? — Refresh the page; scripts may not have loaded.
